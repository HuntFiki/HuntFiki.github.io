I am going to list some of my coping skills and write how they help me.

1. Stop listening or watching the news

Constantly listening to the news and hearing all the horrible things that are happening can be incredibly stressful. So, take a break from the news and do something that makes you happy.

2. Take a hot/ cold shower

Taking a shower is a great way to clear things out. I take hot showers but sometimes I make it cold to shock my system and wake me up.

3. Talk to someone you trust

Talking about your feelings to someone you trust and feel comfortable can really help. You can get another opinion on a situation, that isn't yours, and they can give you some coping skills to help.

4. Journal

Sometimes you may not want to talk to people, so what I recommend doing it writing things down. I have a book with the sole purpose to write my sad thoughts in. It is a good way to process my emotions in the moment.
